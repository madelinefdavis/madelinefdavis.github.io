Project Goals¶

Our final project will focus on Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS). This is a hormonal imbalabce that occurs in your ovaries that causes your hormones to produce unusually high levels of androgen. This is one of the most commone causes of female infertility. This condition is common affect arounf 15% of women of reproductive age. Sympotoms of PCOS often include irregular periods, abnormal hair growth, acne, obesity, darkening of the skin, skin tags, thinning hair and infertility. While you cannot sure PCOs there are ways you can manage symptoms so they are less impactful in day to day life.

The data set we are using can be found here. This data set is a survey of 541 women. It indicates whether or not they have a PCOS diagnosis and whether or not they have some of the common indicators of PCOS. For our final project we will be looking at different symptoms of PCOS to see which are the best indicator of having PCOS and are a sign that an individual should go their doctor to discuss potential treatments.
